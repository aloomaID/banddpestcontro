About Us

B and D Pest Control & Solutions

At B and D Pest Control, we are committed to safeguarding homes and businesses across the United States from unwanted pests. With years of industry experience, our team of certified professionals delivers effective, eco-friendly pest management solutions tailored to each client's unique needs.​ Our services encompass a wide range of pest control solutions, including termite treatment, rodent exclusion, bed bug eradication, and mosquito management. Utilizing the latest technology and environmentally responsible methods, we ensure the safety and comfort of your property while minimizing ecological impact.​ Customer satisfaction is at the heart of our operations. We pride ourselves on prompt response times, transparent communication, and customized treatment plans that address the specific challenges of each situation. Our dedication to excellence has earned us a reputation as a trusted partner in pest control nationwide.

Our Services

We offer a comprehensive range of pest control services tailored to meet the unique needs of our clients:

  • Residential Pest Control: Protecting homes from common pests such as ants, cockroaches, and rodents.
  • Commercial Pest Control: Providing businesses with customized pest management plans to maintain a pest-free environment.
  • Termite Treatment: Implementing effective strategies to detect and eliminate termite infestations.
  • Bed Bug Eradication: Utilizing advanced methods to remove bed bugs and prevent future infestations.
  • Mosquito Control: Reducing mosquito populations to protect against bites and potential diseases.
  • Rodent Exclusion: Identifying and sealing entry points to prevent rodent access.
  • Integrated Pest Management (IPM): Employing a holistic approach that combines multiple strategies for long-term pest control.
